# Project Idea Bin

This is the durable backlog for product or project-specific ideas that are deliberately deferred rather than ready for immediate work. Retrospectives add only approved ideas with a concrete revisit trigger. Never delete history; move resolved items to Closed.

Statuses: `Parked` -> `Ready for Discovery` -> `Promoted` -> `Closed`.

Every active idea records: stable ID and title, Status, Source, Why, Revisit when, and Last considered.
